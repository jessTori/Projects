Horimiya

At first glance, it may seem that Hori is an ordinary teenage girl, but appearance can be deceptive. While the parents are working at work, the heroine is left with all the chores around the house, as well as taking care of her younger brother. Because of her constant employment, she simply does not have time to enjoy the delights of teenage life. Soon she meets Miyamura, who also does not show his real self at school. He seems to be an unfriendly quiet person, but outside the walls of the school he turns out to be a completely different person. So, this couple has a lot in common, and gradually they begin to get closer.

Code Geass: Hangyaku no Lelouch

The story tells about the near future, when half the world was captured by the British Empire. Japan does not exist in this version — it was conquered by Britain years ago and renamed the "11th sector", and the Japanese themselves are pejoratively called the "eleventh". The main character of the story, Lelouch Lamperouge, a disgraced scion of the imperial family, is exiled with his sister to the 11th sector, where he studies at the academy for local aristocrats. By chance, he finds himself involved in an attack by Japanese rebels on Britain. The Empire, which does not tolerate disobedience, immediately sends punitive detachments of soldiers, destroying everything in their path. Fleeing from the Imperials, Lelouch crosses paths with a mysterious green-haired girl who calls herself C.C. Being cornered and surrounded by soldiers, C.S. gives Lelouch a great gift called gias...
